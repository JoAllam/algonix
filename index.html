Who we are

Algonix is a community for students and young people who share a strong interest in technology, programming, and robotics.

But we go beyond learning. At Algonix, members also develop teamwork, creativity, and organizational skills. Every effort counts, and you’ll receive a recognized certificate to showcase your achievements.
